Data Deletion Policy for Savapic

Effective Date: September 27, 2024

At Savapic, we respect your privacy and provide you with the ability to delete your data from our system at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to delete any personal data that we have collected from you, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to: katherinebrooks1989@gmail.com
  • In the subject line, please include: Data Deletion Request
  • In the body of the email, please include the following information:
    • Your full name
    • Your email address associated with the app
    • A request to delete your data

Upon receiving your request, we will delete your data from our system within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

All personal data associated with your use of our app, including but not limited to:

  • Your email address
  • Your Instagram profile information
  • Any media (posts and stories) you have stored through our app
